A Day Their Mouths Will Be Sealed.

the day of judgement, it is known by many names such us the day of resurrection, the day of reckoning, etc.

the day of judgement is a day of fear for all, a day where their mouths will be sealed, and their parts will testify what they used to commit.

On this Day We will seal their mouths, their hands will speak to Us, and their feet will testify to what they used to commit. [36:65]

the criminals, the sinners, the wrongdoers, the disbelievers, they will all suffer intense hardship and thirst, and experience extreme fear, because they will stand for such a long time and will be certain that they will be brought to account, and they will wonder with what Allah, may He be Glorified and Exalted, will requite them.

the day of reckoning wont age like a normal 24 hour day, it will last thousands and thousands of years. on this day, every single human that has ever lived will gather, every single sin of those who disbelieved will be shown. the final decision is whether you go to jahannam (the hellfire), or jannah (the paradise). the kuffar will be thrown into the hellfire, to burn for eternity, filled with regret and remorse.

Losers indeed are those who deny the meeting with Allah until the Hour takes them by surprise, then they will cry, “Woe to us for having ignored this!” They will bear ˹the burden of˺ their sins on their backs. Evil indeed is their burden! [6:31]

one might find these funny, thinking "how might my hands speak! how might my legs testify! impossible, simply impossible." do you think that god almighty is incapable of doing everything? allah is capable of everything, he is all knowing, all seeing, all hearing. if allah made the entire universe with such precise calculations, and made the human so perfectly, would you think he wouldn't be able to simply make things testify?

And say, "The truth is from your Lord, so whoever wills - let him believe; and whoever wills - let him disbelieve." Indeed, We have prepared for the wrongdoers a fire whose walls will surround them. And if they call for relief, they will be relieved with water like murky oil, which scalds [their] faces. Wretched is the drink, and evil is the resting place. [18:29]

on the day, the believers will be entered into paradise, they will have everything they ask for, anything they want, everything they want, whatever they desire, they'll have it. their happiness will never be extinguished, they will never get sick, nor tired, nor sleepy, it will just be pure happiness, unlike the disbelievers.

i have only mentioned a tiny part of the information allah and his prophet have given to us about the day of judgement. read the quran and hadiths for more information.
